Privacy & Data

No servers, no accounts, no analytics.

On-Device

  • Apple Voice: All processing on your Mac. Nothing leaves the device.
  • Settings: Local UserDefaults + iCloud Key-Value sync.
  • API keys: Mac Keychain only. Never sent to VoxClaw.

Third-Party Voices

When you enable OpenAI or ElevenLabs, the text being read is sent to that provider. Nothing else — no name, email, or device info.

Listener

Port 4140 accepts local/network connections only. Nothing exposed to the internet.
