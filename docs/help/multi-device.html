Multi-Device

Relay speech from your Mac to iPhone or another Mac.

How It Works

VoxClaw uses Bonjour to discover peers on your local network. Your Mac relays text to all enabled peers.

Setup

  1. Install VoxClaw on both devices
  2. Same Wi-Fi network
  3. Settings › Peers — toggle on the device

Cross-Device Interruption

Type on your Mac and all peers stop speaking and play the interrupt sound.
