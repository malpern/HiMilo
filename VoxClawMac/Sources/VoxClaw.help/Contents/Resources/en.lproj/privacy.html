Privacy & Data

VoxClaw is designed to keep your data local. We don't run servers, don't have accounts, and don't collect analytics.

Data That Stays on Your Mac

  • Apple Built-in Voice: All speech processing happens on-device via macOS AVSpeechSynthesizer. No text leaves your Mac.
  • Settings: Stored in local UserDefaults and synced via iCloud Key-Value Storage to your other Apple devices.
  • API keys: Stored in your Mac's Keychain. Never transmitted to VoxClaw or any server other than the voice provider you selected.

Data Sent to Third Parties

Only when you explicitly enable OpenAI or ElevenLabs voices:

  • What is sent: The text being read aloud, to the voice provider's API
  • What is not sent: Your name, email, device identifiers, usage data, or any personal information
  • When: Only at the moment text is spoken with a third-party engine enabled

No Data Collection

VoxClaw does not collect, store, or transmit analytics, crash reports, device identifiers, location data, or advertising identifiers. We have no servers and no way to access your data.

Network Listener

The HTTP listener on port 4140 accepts connections only from your local machine or local network. It does not expose any data to the internet.
