Multi-Device

VoxClaw can relay speech to other VoxClaw instances on your network. When your agent speaks on your Mac, your iPhone (or another Mac) can speak it too.

How It Works

VoxClaw uses Bonjour to discover other VoxClaw instances on your local network. When your Mac receives text from an agent, it relays the text to all enabled peer devices.

Setup

  1. Install VoxClaw on both devices
  2. Make sure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network
  3. Open Settings › Peers on your Mac
  4. Your other device should appear automatically via Bonjour
  5. Toggle it on to enable relay

Peer Controls

In the Peers section of Settings:

  • Toggle — enable or disable relay to each peer
  • Test — send a test phrase to verify the connection
  • Resolving... — the peer was discovered but its address hasn't resolved yet; wait a moment

Cross-Device Interruption

When you interrupt speech by typing on your Mac, VoxClaw sends an acknowledgment to all peer devices. They stop speaking and play the interrupt sound, keeping everything in sync.
